Multimodal Affective Pedagogical Agents for Different Types of Learners

针对不同学生的多模态虚拟教学动画系统

2018-2021 | Nicoletta Adamo, Purdue (PI), Richard E. Mayer, UCSB (co-PI), Bedrich Benes, Purdue (co-PI)

NSF - IIS - Cyberlearning, award # 1821894, Purdue Instructional Innovation Grant

My Role as Research Assistant is to tackle the technical difficulties in this project, including character rig building, motion capture data targeting and procedural animation development. I've created four characters (Malcolm, Luna, David, Dana) with features including auto lip sync, emotion based facial expression, IK controlled feet and eye gaze, Mecanim based animation state machine, voice beat detection and more.

 

A Comparison Between Different Interpolation Methods for Character Upper Body Animation

硕士论文：对比不同插值算法下的人物上身动画

2019-2020 | Xingyu Lei

Purdue Computer Graphics Technology Master's Thesis

This thesis compares the naturalness of character animation using three interpolations: Linear Euler interpolation, Spherical Linear Quaternion interpolation and Spherical Spline Quaternion interpolation. This thesis discusses the mathematical equations, motion curves and velocity graphs as well as the perceptional aspect of evaluating these interpolations on upper-body character animation. Participants in this experiment evaluate how natural an animation looks using three different test methods: Two-Alternative Forced Choice, Yes/No and Rating.
